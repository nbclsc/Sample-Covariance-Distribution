Show that the distribution of $(n-1)\textbf{S}$ is a Wishart distribution with $n-1$ degrees of freedom.

$$
(n-1)
\textbf{S}
\sim
W_{p}
\left(n-1, \mathbf{\Sigma}\right)
$$
